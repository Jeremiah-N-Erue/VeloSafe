The Problem

Urban Mobility in Lagos is Broken

Despite explosive smartphone adoption and a growing middle class, Lagos riders face a market defined by danger, opacity, and distrust.

🚨
73%

Riders fear for safety

Nearly three-quarters of ride-hailing users in Lagos report at least one safety incident or near-miss. Driver identity fraud and route deviations remain pervasive.

⚡
3–5×

Surge pricing unpredictability

Competitors levy aggressive surge multipliers during peak hours, eroding rider trust and pricing out lower-income commuters on essential corridors.

🚗
35%+

Driver churn from high commissions

Bolt and Uber extract 25–30% from every fare. Financially squeezed drivers moonlight illegally or abandon platforms entirely, degrading service quality.

Our Solution

AI-Powered Safety, Built for Lagos

VeloSafe reimagines ride-hailing from the ground up — with technology that verifies every driver, monitors every trip, and prices every fare transparently.

🛡️

AI Driver Verification

Multi-layer biometric and document verification before any driver enters the network. Continuous in-trip monitoring flags anomalous behavior in real time.

📡

Real-Time Safety Monitoring

Live GPS tracking, SOS triggers, and automated alerts to emergency contacts. Riders share trip details with a tap. Incidents escalate to our safety desk instantly.

💰

Transparent, Flat-Rate Pricing

No surge multipliers. Riders see the final fare upfront. Our dynamic pricing algorithm optimises driver utilisation without penalising riders.

🤝

20% Driver Commission

The lowest commission structure in the Lagos market. Higher driver earnings mean lower churn, better service quality, and a loyal, growing supply side.
